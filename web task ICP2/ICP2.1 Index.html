BANGTAN SEONYEONDAN

BANGTAN SEONYEONDAN are known as BTS, which is the most popular kpop band On the earth.The name signifies the group's desire "to block out stereotypes, criticisms, and expectations that aim on adolescents like bullets".BTS's music is also a success because of the subject of their music. While many K-pop songs are about relationships and love, BTS covers subjects that other bands may not like bullying, elitism, and mental health. Their ability to be relatable, humble while still being entertaining is rare.
